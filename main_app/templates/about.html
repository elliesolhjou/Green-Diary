About Green Diary

Green Diary is a dedicated platform aimed at raising awareness about the environmental impact of automotive emissions. We believe in the power of information and the role it plays in guiding responsible decision-making. Our CO2 tracker allows individuals to measure the carbon footprint of their journeys, with each trip's data contributing to a broader understanding of personal and collective ecological impact.

Our innovative system not only calculates the emissions but also equates them to the number of trees needed to offset the impact, offering a tangible perspective on sustainability. By integrating advanced APIs, Green Diary simplifies the process of vehicle data collection and emission calculations, ensuring accuracy and ease of use.

Developed with Django and Bootstrap, Green Diary features a robust and user-friendly interface that makes tracking your environmental impact both simple and effective. Our goal is to empower users with the knowledge to make greener choices and to foster a community dedicated to reducing carbon footprints, one trip at a time.
